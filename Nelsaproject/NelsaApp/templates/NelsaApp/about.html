Our story

Built on trust, driven by people

“King of the Road” is more than a slogan — it is our standard for punctuality, vehicle care, and respectful service on every departure.

Founded in 1989, GARANTI EXPRESS has grown into a trusted name in Cameroon’s interurban transport sector. We connect communities across the country with modern coaches maintained for long-distance comfort and safety.

Today, passengers can plan trips online, choose seats, and pay securely — while our terminal teams continue the hands-on support that has defined us for decades.
